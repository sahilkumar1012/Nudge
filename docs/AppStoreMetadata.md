## Description (4000 chars max)

Nudge makes sure you never miss a meeting. It reads your calendar events and triggers loud, full-screen alarms — powered by Apple's AlarmKit — that fire even through Do Not Disturb and Silent Mode.

**How it works:**
1. Grant calendar access
2. Nudge automatically syncs your events
3. Get a real alarm before every meeting — just like your morning alarm

**Key Features:**
• Real alarms, not notifications — fires through Do Not Disturb and Focus modes
• Snooze support — snooze just like your morning alarm
• Configurable lead time — alert at event time, or 1/5/10/15/30 minutes before
• Smart grouping — events at the same time trigger one alarm, not five
• Per-event control — mute events you don't need reminders for
• Morning auto-sync — refreshes your calendar daily, even if the app isn't open
• Fully offline — no internet, no accounts, no tracking
• Spotlight search — find Nudge when you search "calendar" or "meeting"

**Why Nudge?**
Standard calendar notifications are easy to miss — they're silent, brief, and disappear into your notification stack. Nudge replaces them with real alarms that demand your attention, so you're never the person who joins 10 minutes late.

**Privacy first:**
Nudge runs entirely on your device. No data collection, no analytics, no ads, no accounts. Your calendar stays yours.

## App Review Notes
Nudge requires calendar access to function. To test:
1. Launch the app and grant Calendar and Alarm permissions
2. Create a calendar event 10 minutes from now
3. Tap the sync button — an alarm will be scheduled
4. Wait for the alarm to fire — it will show a full-screen dismiss/snooze interface

The app uses AlarmKit (iOS 26+) and requires a physical device for full alarm testing. Alarms may not fire correctly in the Simulator.
