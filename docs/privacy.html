Privacy Policy

Nudge for iOS · Effective March 30, 2026

Overview

Nudge is a calendar alarm app that runs entirely on your device. Your privacy is fundamental to how we built it — we do not collect, store, or transmit any of your data.

Data We Access

Nudge requests access to the following with your explicit permission:

  • Calendar Events — Read-only access to your device calendars via Apple's EventKit framework. Used solely to display events and schedule alarms.
  • Notifications & Alarms — Used to schedule AlarmKit alarms that alert you before meetings.

Data We Collect

None. Nudge does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking
  • No crash reporting to external services
  • No advertising or ad identifiers
  • No user accounts or sign-in
  • No network requests — the app works fully offline

Data Storage

All preferences (lead time, snooze duration, muted events) are stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. Calendar data is read on-demand from the system and is never cached or persisted by Nudge.

Third-Party Services

Nudge uses no third-party SDKs, services, or APIs. It relies exclusively on Apple's native frameworks: EventKit, AlarmKit, UserNotifications, and CoreSpotlight.

Children's Privacy

Nudge does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not require any personal information to function.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
